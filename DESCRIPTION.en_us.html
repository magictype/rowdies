Rowdies is a Latin display typeface inspired by the rough & tough Indian action cinema. Designed by Jaikishan Patel for Action, Drama, Thriller, Noir, Crime, Adventure genres.

The font family includes 3 weights. Light: designed to set supporting body-text with optimal glyphs width. Regular: designed for the large headers & titles. Bold: designed for extremely heavyweight title designs.

To contribute, see https://github.com/magictype/rowdies
