Rowdies is a Latin display typeface inspired by the rough & tough Indian action cinema. Roughness and oddness of each individual letter contributes collectively as a typeface to the fantesy of being bold, fearless and strong. Designed by Jaikishan Patel for action, drama, adventure, thriller, noir & crime genres of storytelling

The font family includes 600+ glyphs in 3 weights; light,regular and bold

To contribute, see https://github.com/magictype/rowdies
